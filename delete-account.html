Delete Your Account

App: Sumo Fantasy

You can delete your Sumo Fantasy account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Sumo Fantasy app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab (tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your profile information (email address, display name, username)
  • Your fantasy team selections and history
  • Your league memberships and standings
  • Your scores and career statistics
  • Your push notification token (if enabled)
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including fantasy teams, league memberships, scores, and career stats, will be permanently deleted. There is no additional retention period.

Alternative: Contact Us

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, you can request account deletion by emailing us.

Email: sumofantasyapp@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Sumo Fantasy account. We will process your request within 7 business days.
